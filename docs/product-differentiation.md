# Optimizio Performance - Product Differentiation

## Why Optimizio is Different
Traditional tools often list problems but do not explain them clearly or help teams know what to do next.

Optimizio stands out by:
- explaining issues in plain language
- prioritizing fixes by impact and effort
- generating actionable recommendations
- comparing websites against competitors
- combining technical auditing with AI guidance

## Core Promise
Optimizio turns website audits into an intelligent optimization workflow instead of a static report.
